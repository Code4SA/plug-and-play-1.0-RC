2013 Nominee: Poderopedia.org

El Innovator

  • La Sabiduría es Poder, Knowledge is Power. Few understand this statement like the staff at Poderopedia. This website is an editorial and crowd sourced data journalism platform that creates a map of the top businessmen and political figures in Chile. Using data visualization and semantic technology, Poderopedia has provided Chilenos with a more transparent and insightful view into the tactics of the influential decision makers who impact their community, and will soon expand to other parts of Latin America.
